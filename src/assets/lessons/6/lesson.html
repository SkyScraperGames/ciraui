Introduction to Conditional Statements

Conditional statements help the program make a choice. Conditional statements are made of if and else blocks.
Pretend you are trying to decide if you should wear a rain coat. You would look outside before making the choice to put on your rain coat. This choice can be made into a conditional statement. If: It is raining I will wear my rain coat. Else: Otherwise I will not wear my rain coat.

For you to do

Use if and the print command to print a message for when there are more wizards and dragons than townsfolk. Use else to print a different message for when there are more townsfolk than wizards and dragons!
